# Stage 1: Market Research - MCP Skills Server

## Owners
- **Product Manager** (Lead)
- **Data Analyst**
- **Marketing Manager**

## Overview

This stage conducts comprehensive market research and competitive analysis for the MCP Skills Server project. The research validates the problem identified in Stage 0 and provides data-driven insights into the market opportunity, competitive landscape, and pricing strategies.

## Research Objectives

1. Validate that the problem (AI agents lacking skills support) is real and significant
2. Understand the MCP ecosystem market size, trends, and growth projections
3. Analyze 5-7 direct and indirect competitors
4. Identify market gaps and unique opportunities
5. Research pricing models and monetization strategies
6. Gather evidence supporting market demand

## Key Findings Summary

### Market Opportunity
- **Market Size**: AI development workflow automation tools market valued at $15-30 billion in 2025
- **MCP Ecosystem**: Close to 2,000 MCP servers in the registry (407% growth from September 2025)
- **Industry Adoption**: OpenAI (March 2025), Google DeepMind (April 2025), and major enterprise backing

### Competitive Landscape
- Analyzed 7 competitors across MCP servers, AI agent frameworks, and development workflow tools
- Key gap: No existing MCP server specifically for exposing structured development workflows as skills
- Most MCP servers focus on data access and tool execution, not workflow orchestration

### Market Validation
- Strong demand for interoperability in AI development tools
- Security concerns present opportunity for differentiation
- Growing need for standardized development methodologies across AI platforms
